About Me

Dr. Yanni Yang obtained her Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computing in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2021, and worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the The Hong Kong Polytechnic University from 2021 to 2022. During 2019-2020, she visit Media Lab in MIT.
